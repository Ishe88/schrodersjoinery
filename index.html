Story by Schröder's Joinery

Are you looking for custom made designs?
We make quality hand-crafted kitchens,
cabinetry & bespoke furniture.

Our products made just for you. Schröder's Joinery was established in 2005 by two brothers, Keith and Graham Schröder.

The business continues to be owner operated. Every project is personally supervised to maintain the highest quality for our clients.

Our business is focused towards creating custom made kitchens, furniture and cabinetry — conceived with precision, built with intent.
